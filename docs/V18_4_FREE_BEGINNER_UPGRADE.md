# IronPanel v18.4 — Free Beginner and Upgrade Center

- Fresh installations start as `beginer` automatically.
- No license key is requested by the installer.
- The panel remains operational without LicensePanel.
- `/upgrade` activates Plus, Pro or Admin licenses and displays a feature comparison.
- Removing, expiring or rejecting a paid key returns the effective feature set to Beginner.
- Temporary license-server outages preserve the most recently valid paid tier for up to 24 hours, then safely fall back to Beginner.
- Legacy `/license` routes redirect to the new upgrade flow.
